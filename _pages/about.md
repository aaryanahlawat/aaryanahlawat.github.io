My name is Aaryan Ahlawat, and I am an undergraduate student at the University of California, Santa Cruz, pursuing a B.S. in Computer Engineering with a current GPA of 3.97 (as of Summer 2025). I am passionate about designing robust network systems, integrating hardware and software, and exploring secure, scalable computing solutions.

Interests
======
My academic interests include distributed systems, applied cryptography, data privacy, and sustainable computing. I enjoy tackling interdisciplinary problems that bridge low-level hardware design and high-level software architecture.

Hobbies
======
Outside of academics, I enjoy building personal electronics projects, participating in hackathons, and learning about space systems and emerging technologies. In my free time, I also like to learn new things, explore the outdoors, and contribute to cultural and community organizations on campus. I've recently gotten into 3D printing and hope to make some cool fantasy things. I've also am actively associated with the video games community.
