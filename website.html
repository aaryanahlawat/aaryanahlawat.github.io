Real-time guitar-to-piano timbre transfer pipeline using YIN f0 extraction, RMS loudness, and MFCC features fed into a 4-layer MLP. 64-harmonic additive synthesizer with phase-continuous output at ~11ms total latency on consumer hardware. Generalizes across guitar styles with no retraining required.
